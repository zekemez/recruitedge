Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about RecruitEdge AI

How long does it take to see results?

Most athletes start getting coach responses within the first 2 to 3 weeks. Results depend on your athlete's profile and the programs targeted, but persistence is key. Our AI follows up automatically every 5 to 7 days so coaches don't forget about your athlete.

Will coaches know it's AI?

Our messages are personalized for each program and sound completely natural. Coaches care about finding talent, not whether you typed the email yourself. Many families already use recruiting services and consultants. We're just more efficient and affordable than traditional methods.

What if my kid isn't a top recruit?

Perfect. That's exactly who we help most. Top recruits already get plenty of coach attention. Our AI levels the playing field by making sure your athlete's name stays in front of coaches at schools that actually match their skill level. We target realistic programs where they can actually play.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. All plans are month to month with no contracts or commitments. You can cancel whenever you want. Most families stay because they're getting results, but you're never locked in. If you cancel, your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Do I need to provide the coach list?

No. We build the entire list based on your athlete's profile, academic standing, location preferences, and division targets. You can add specific schools you want us to prioritize, but we'll automatically recommend programs that are realistic matches based on our database of thousands of college programs.

What sports do you support?

We support all NCAA sports including lacrosse, soccer, baseball, softball, basketball, football, volleyball, swimming, track and field, tennis, golf, hockey, wrestling, and more. Our AI is trained on recruiting patterns across all sports and divisions.

Is this NCAA compliant?

Yes, absolutely. We're reaching out on behalf of your athlete, which is completely allowed under NCAA rules. We don't make promises to coaches, don't violate dead periods, and everything we send is factual. It's the same as if you emailed coaches yourself, just automated and more persistent.

How do I know which coaches responded?

Your dashboard shows real time updates. You'll get instant notifications when coaches open your emails, click your highlight links, or respond. We track everything so you know exactly where you stand with each program. You can see which coaches are interested and which ones need more follow up.

How much does this cost compared to other services?

Traditional recruiting services charge $3,000 to $10,000+ for the same outreach we automate. We're a fraction of the cost at $20 to $100 per month, and you get better results because our AI never gets tired of following up. You're paying for the technology, not someone's hourly rate.

When should my athlete start using this?

Most athletes start junior year, but some sports recruit earlier. Sophomores can start for early commitment sports like lacrosse and soccer. The key is starting before your athlete's senior year when most roster spots are already filled. Earlier is always better in recruiting.

What if I don't have highlight footage yet?

No problem. You can start building your profile and we'll begin outreach once your highlights are ready. We also offer professional highlight tape editing as part of our Pro and Elite plans, or as a standalone service for $75. Just send us your raw game footage and we'll turn it into a coach ready tape.

Do you help with official visits and campus tours?

Yes! Our Elite plan includes help coordinating official visits. We'll help you schedule multiple visits efficiently, prepare questions to ask coaches, and make sure you're making the most of each campus visit. We can also help you understand scholarship offers and compare programs.

What happens if my athlete gets injured?

You can pause your account at any time. If your athlete gets injured and needs to take time off, just let us know and we'll pause outreach until they're ready to return. Your profile stays active and you can resume anytime without losing any data or progress.

Can you help with D3 schools that don't give athletic scholarships?

Absolutely. D3 schools offer academic scholarships and need talented athletes just as much as D1 and D2 programs. We target D3 schools based on your athlete's academic profile and help them understand the full financial aid package available, which often equals or exceeds athletic scholarships at other levels.

How is this different from just emailing coaches myself?

The difference is consistency and volume. Most parents send 10 to 20 emails and give up when they don't hear back. Our AI sends 100+ emails per month and follows up persistently with every coach. Coaches are busy and often need multiple touches before they respond. We make sure your athlete stays top of mind without you having to manage hundreds of follow ups manually.
